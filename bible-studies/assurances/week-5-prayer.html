What is Prayer?

+++We discussed that prayer is entering God's powerful presence, a place of refuge, and that prayer is one of the main ways we build our personal relationship with God (but remember, God is God, a.k.a. He's not just our friend; He is sovereign and all-mighty!

+++We also discussed that prayer is a form of worship that we can do daily, and multiple times a day. Unlike a Liturgical service, which if offered 1 or 2 times a week, we can pray any day at any time!

  • —After reading

John 16:23 & Matthew 28:19

, we decided that we start and end our prayers in the name of the Holy Trinity (Father, Son, and Holy Spirit), pray In Jesus Christ's name, and that we direct our prayers to the Holy Trinity. Most times we pray to God the Father, sometimes to Jesus Himself, and sometimes to the Holy Spirit. Either way, we are praying to God all the same!
